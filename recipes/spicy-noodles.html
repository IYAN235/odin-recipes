Easy Spicy Noodles

Description

This chicken noodle soup recipe can be on the table in just 40 minutes! Whether
you're feeling under the weather or you're just in need of a little comfort, this
comforting soup will warm you up from the inside out.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • ½ cup chopped onion
  • ½ cup chopped celery
  • 4 cup chicken broth
  • 1 cup vegetable broth
  • ½ pound chopped cooked chicken breast
  • 1 ½ cups egg noodles
  • 1 cup sliced carrots
  • ½ teaspoon dried basil
  • ½ teaspoon dried oregano
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Melt butter in a large pot over medium heat. Add onion and celery and cook
    until just tender, about 5 minutes.
  2. Add chicken broth, vegetable broth, chicken, egg noodles, carrots, basil,
    oregano, salt, and pepper. Stir to combine and bring to a boil.
  3. Reduce heat and simmer for 20 minutes.
  4. Now Serve
